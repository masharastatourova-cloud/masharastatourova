Accelerator Program

The Challenge

Running an accelerator program means managing a dense, fast-moving environment with many stakeholders: founders at different stages, mentors with varied availability, investors, staff, and program partners, all moving simultaneously.

The operational infrastructure needed to hold all of it together without creating unnecessary friction for the founders it was designed to serve.

My Role

I supported program operations across the full arc of the cohort, helping translate the program's ambitions into day-to-day execution.

  • End-to-end program logistics and scheduling
  • Mentor coordination and relationship management
  • Founder support across the cohort
  • Stakeholder communication and updates
  • Event and demo day preparation
  • Internal systems and process design

The Outcome

A well-run program that allowed founders to focus on building rather than navigating logistics and confirmed that operational clarity is just as valuable inside startup ecosystems as anywhere else.
